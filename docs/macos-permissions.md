# macOS Permissions for Nebula Client

To capture and inject keyboard/mouse events on macOS:

1. Open **System Settings**.
2. Go to **Privacy & Security**.
3. Enable your Nebula client app in:
   - **Accessibility**
   - **Input Monitoring** (if required by capture path)

After changing permissions, restart the client process.

If permission prompts do not appear:
- Remove app entry from Accessibility/Input Monitoring.
- Restart app and re-authorize.
